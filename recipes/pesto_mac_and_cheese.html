Pesto Mac & Cheese

Description

Pesto Mac and Cheese with Chicken – elbow macaroni and bites of chicken in a creamy, cheese sauce flavored with garden-fresh basil pesto. This simple dish cooks completely in ONE pot!

Ingredients

Serves 4

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 tbps flour
  • 2 cups full fat milk
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 1 1/4 cups chicken stock
  • 2 cups macaroni
  • 2 cups cheese of choice
  • 1/2 cup pesto
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cut chicken breast into smaller pieces.
  2. Heat olive oil on a pan. Add chicken breast and salt and pepper to taste. Cook for about 8 minuts, then remove from pan.
  3. Heat butter in the same pan for 3 minutes.
  4. Slowly add the milk, whisking over medium heat.
  5. When the mixture is bubbly, add water, stock and season generously with salt and pepper.
  6. Pour in the macaroni. Reduce the heat to medium-low, cover and allow pasta to cook for 8 to 10 minutes until the pasta is al dente
  7. Remove the pan from heat. Slowly add in the cheese, combining thoroughly before adding more. Add in pesto and fold it in with chicken and pasta.
