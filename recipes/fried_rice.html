Salmon Fried Rice

Description

With just a handful of ingredients – leftover rice, salted salmon, eggs and scallions, you can cook up this delicious one-pan Salmon Fried Rice. Plenty of flavor to a meal in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

Serves 2

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 spring onions, chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 bowl of rice
  • 2 tbsp teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tbsp Kewpie mayo
  • 2 tsp honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in pan. Sear the salmon fillets for 6 minutes with the skin side down.
  2. Flip the salmon and continue to fry for 3 minutes. Add spring onions and soy sauce into the pan. Shred the salmon with the spatula whilst stirring. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Add eggs and scramble. Add rice and honey. Mix well
  4. Serve fried rice in a bowl. Garnish with Kewpie mayo and spring onions.
